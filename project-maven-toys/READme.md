# 📈 Inventory & Revenue Growth Analysis (Maven Toys)
> **Impact:** Identified strategies to recapture <mark><b>$3.7M in lost revenue</b></mark> and achieve a <mark><b>10% growth target</b></mark>.

## 📌 Project Summary
This project analyzes the Mexico Toy Sales dataset to identify key factors for achieving a 10% revenue growth target. Despite a global market upswing, Maven Toys experienced a significant revenue decline. My analysis focuses on rectifying inventory imbalances—specifically **stockouts and overstock**—to secure a **$14M revenue stream**.

---

## 🎯 Goals & Objectives
* **Revenue Recovery:** Recapture <mark><b>$3.7M</b></mark> in lost revenue caused by systemic availability gaps.
* **Growth Target:** Increase total revenue by **10%** within six months through targeted promotional investment.
* **Proactive Planning:** Mitigate a forecasted seasonal slump identified for September.

---

## 🚀 Achievements (AQS Framework)
* **Analyzed** 829,000+ sales rows to identify a **$3.7M revenue leakage** caused by inventory stockouts.
* **Developed** a tiered pricing strategy revealing that the **Medium Price Tier** delivers a **33.7% profit margin**.
* **Engineered** a **Python Prophet forecast model** that identified a critical September sales dip, enabling a proactive recovery strategy.
* **Optimized** supply chain efficiency by identifying **"Zombie" inventory** (104 weeks of supply) and recommending stock transfers.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Methods
### A. Tools
- **Language:** Python (Pandas) for data cleaning and merging 829k+ rows.
- **Forecasting:** Prophet library for time-series demand prediction.
- **Visualization:** Tableau for executive dashboards and regional mapping.

### B. Methods
- **Price Tier Segmentation:** Categorizing products (High, Medium, Low) to evaluate margin impact.
- **Inventory Status Analysis:** Calculating **"Weeks of Supply"** and turnover rates.
- **EDA:** Evaluating holiday promotional effectiveness and pricing tiers.
- **Time-Series Forecasting:** Predicting **180 days** of future sales activity.

## 💡 Final Recommendations
1. **Inventory Realignment:** Reallocate "Zombie" stock to top-performing cities like **Mexico City**.
2. **Product Focus:** Prioritize **Colorbuds** (Electronics) as they are the strongest profit drivers at <mark><b>$834K</b></mark>.
3. **Marketing:** Implement a **"September Recovery Promo"** to counteract the forecasted seasonal decline.
